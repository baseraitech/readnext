Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

ReadNext ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All your book data, notes, and progress are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising and Google Books API for fetching book details. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data according to their own policies.

3. Data Security

Since your data is stored locally, it is as secure as your device. We provide manual backup/restore via JSON files which you control.
